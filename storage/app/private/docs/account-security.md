# Account Security

## Password Requirements
- Minimum 12 characters
- Must include uppercase, lowercase, a number, and a symbol
- Cannot match any of your last 5 passwords

## Two-Factor Authentication
We strongly recommend enabling 2FA on your account.
Supported methods:
- Authenticator apps (Google Authenticator, Authy, 1Password)
- SMS codes (less secure but available)
- Hardware security keys (YubiKey, etc.)

## Account Recovery
If you cannot access your account:
1. Use the "Forgot Password" link on the sign-in page
2. Check your spam folder for the reset email
3. If 2FA is locking you out, use a backup code or contact support

## Suspicious Activity
If you notice login attempts you don't recognize:
- Change your password immediately
- Sign out of all active sessions from your account settings
- Enable 2FA if you haven't already
- Email security@example.com with the details

## Data and Privacy
You can download or delete your account data from Settings → Privacy.
Account deletion is permanent after a 30-day grace period.
