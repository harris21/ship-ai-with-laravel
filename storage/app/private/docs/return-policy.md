# Return Policy

## Standard Returns
Items can be returned within 30 days of delivery for a full refund.
Items must be in unused condition with original packaging and tags.

## Damaged Items
If you receive a damaged or defective item, you can return it at any time.
Contact support within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the damage.
We will provide a prepaid return label and offer either:
- A full refund processed within 5-7 business days
- A replacement item shipped via express delivery at no cost

## Non-Returnable Items
The following cannot be returned:
- Gift cards and digital products
- Personalized or custom-made items
- Perishable goods
- Items marked as final sale

## Refund Processing
Refunds are issued to the original payment method.
Standard refunds: 5-7 business days after item is received.
Express refunds (for damaged items): 2-3 business days.
