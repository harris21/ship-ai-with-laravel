# Product Warranty

## Standard Warranty
All products include a 1-year limited warranty against manufacturing defects.
The warranty period starts on the date of delivery.

## What Is Covered
- Manufacturing defects in materials or workmanship
- Component failures under normal use
- Functional issues not caused by user damage

## What Is Not Covered
- Cosmetic wear from normal use
- Damage from accidents, drops, or liquid exposure
- Damage from unauthorized repairs or modifications
- Loss or theft

## Filing a Warranty Claim
1. Contact support with your order number and a description of the issue
2. Include photos or video if the defect is visible
3. Our team responds within 1 business day with next steps
4. If approved, we send a prepaid shipping label for the return

## Warranty Resolution
For approved claims we offer:
- Repair (typically 7-10 business days)
- Replacement with the same or an equivalent product
- Store credit for the original purchase amount

## Extended Warranty
A 2-year extended warranty is available at checkout for select products.
Extended warranty must be purchased within 30 days of the original order.
