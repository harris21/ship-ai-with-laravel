# Shipping Guide

## Shipping Options
- **Standard**: 5-7 business days. Free on orders over $50, otherwise $5.99.
- **Express**: 2-3 business days. $12.99 flat rate.
- **Overnight**: Next business day. $24.99 (orders placed before 2 PM EST).

## International Shipping
We ship to over 60 countries.
- Standard International: 10-15 business days, rates calculated at checkout.
- Express International: 5-7 business days, rates calculated at checkout.
- Customs fees and import duties are the responsibility of the recipient.

## Order Processing
Orders placed before 2 PM EST on business days ship the same day.
Orders placed after 2 PM, on weekends, or on holidays ship the next business day.

## Tracking
A tracking number is emailed once your order ships.
You can also track orders from your account dashboard at any time.

## Delivery Issues
If your package is marked delivered but you haven't received it:
- Wait 24 hours — carriers occasionally scan packages as delivered early
- Check with neighbors, household members, and your building's mail room
- Contact support if it doesn't arrive within 48 hours of the delivery notification
