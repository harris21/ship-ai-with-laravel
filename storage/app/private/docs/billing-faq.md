# Billing FAQ

## Accepted Payment Methods
- Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover
- PayPal
- Apple Pay and Google Pay
- Store credit and gift cards

## When You Are Charged
Your card is authorized at checkout but only charged when the order ships.
For pre-orders, you are charged when the item becomes available.

## Subscription Billing
Subscription orders renew automatically on the same day each month.
You can pause, skip, or cancel subscriptions from your account at any time.
Cancellations made at least 48 hours before renewal apply to the next cycle.

## Failed Payments
If a payment fails, we retry it twice over the following 5 days.
You will receive an email with instructions to update your payment method.

## Duplicate Charges
If you see two charges for the same order, one is most likely an authorization hold.
Authorization holds drop off automatically within 3-5 business days.
If both charges remain after a week, contact support with the transaction IDs.

## Sales Tax
Sales tax is calculated based on your shipping address.
Tax-exempt customers can submit documentation to billing@example.com.
